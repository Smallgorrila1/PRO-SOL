PRO-SOL

Entrepreneurship Event

More About PRO-SOL

PRO-SOL - it would be a contest where participants would be asked to identify loopholes or problems of companies and give a solution to it by using their "Entrepreneurship skill"

How It Would Be Conducted

A list of companies would be selected and then contacted about this event and for prizes.The Candidate would be allotted a comany. They have to complete the task in given time. The juries would select the winner

Prizes

Winner would get a certificae along with a memento.If companies agree, the winner would get a chance to have an internship in that company

Requirements

Permission from autority, Time slot for stage, Judges, Human resource

Conclusion

This event would give a chance to student to apply their analytical and academical knowledge to real problems. It would also allow student to showcase their Entrepreneurship talent and get a reward out of it.
